# Research

## Purpose

The `research` directory contains technical investigations, experiments, feasibility studies, benchmarks, proof-of-concepts, and design explorations that inform the evolution of XeniosAI.

Research documents are exploratory in nature and do not represent accepted architecture until formalized through an ADR or architecture document.

## Planned Contents

- AI Model Evaluations
- LLM Benchmarks
- Technology Comparisons
- Performance Experiments
- Integration Feasibility Studies
- UX Research
- Security Research
- Cost Analysis
- Vendor Evaluations

## Design Principles

- Research should be reproducible.
- Record assumptions and methodology.
- Clearly distinguish facts from hypotheses.
- Reference supporting data where possible.
- Promote mature findings into ADRs or architecture documents when adopted.
