## Core Rules
- Documentation before code.
- AI never owns business logic.
- Business services expose tools/APIs.
- Every architectural decision must answer:
  - Would this work for 10,000 properties?
  - Is the component replaceable?
  - Can a new engineer understand it from the repository?
